# NITS - Neuro-Oncological Integrated Treatment System

## Welcome to the NITS Website

This is the official website for **NITS (Neuro-Oncological Integrated Treatment System)** - an advanced medical technology that combines multiple imaging devices into one integrated system to deliver faster, more precise cancer treatment.

### 🎯 Our Mission

**Why spend all day in the hospital when treatment can be completed by noon?**

NITS revolutionizes cancer treatment by integrating X-ray, CT, MRI, and Angiogram technologies into a single, unified machine, reducing treatment time and improving patient outcomes.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **75% Reduction in Treatment Time** - Complete treatment by noon instead of full day
- **One Integrated Device** - X-ray, CT, MRI, and Angiogram combined
- **60% Less Radiation Exposure** - Minimized radiation through optimization
- **90% Precision Improvement** - Enhanced accuracy in tumor targeting
- **95% Patient Satisfaction** - Real results from real patients
